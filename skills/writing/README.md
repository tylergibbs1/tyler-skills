# Writing

Copy skills that keep product writing accurate and human-sounding. All model-invoked.

- **[human-sounding-copy](./human-sounding-copy/SKILL.md)**: Make writing read as a specific human wrote it. Cuts AI verbosity, strips the lexical, punctuation, and formatting tells, then rebuilds with cohesion, rhythm, concreteness, stakes, and voice. Reference files carry the tell catalog, a list of exemplar writers to study before drafting, the craft canon, the idiolect method, and the research base.
